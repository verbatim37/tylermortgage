Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the home loan process.

What is the difference between a mortgage broker and a bank?

A bank only offers its own products, whereas a mortgage broker has access to a wide variety of lenders and products. This allows a mortgage broker to shop for the best rate and terms for you, often leading to better deals than you can get from a single bank. We are a team of loan professionals who have access to numerous lenders, allowing us to help you find the best loan to fit your needs, at a competitive rate.

What are the key steps in the home buying process?

The process generally involves these key steps: getting pre-qualified, finding a home, making an offer, securing financing (that's where we come in!), and finally, closing on your new home. Our team will guide you through each of these stages, ensuring a smooth and stress-free experience.

How long does the pre-qualification process take?

Getting pre-qualified is the first and fastest step in the process. It involves a quick review of your financial situation to determine how much you can afford to borrow. This can often be done in a single day, giving you a clear picture of your purchasing power so you can start looking for homes with confidence.
